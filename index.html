Methodology

    NDGR is the indicator to measure the network approach to geospatial reference.

    Value of 0 to all articles that use a coordinate template in their headlines

    Value of 1 if there is at least one link within the full text of this article that connects to an article with NDGR 0

    Value of  2 is constituted if an article has at least one link pointing to an article with NDGR 1
